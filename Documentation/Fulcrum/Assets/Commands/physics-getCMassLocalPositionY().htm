float FulcrumPhy::getCMassLocalPositionY ( int id )

This returns the centre of mass local position along the y-axis of the given actor, the centre of mass is not necessarily the centre of the actor depending on what shapes the actor contains and their local positions in the frame of the actor.

Parameters:
int id - Object/Actor id.


Returns:
float - Centre of mass local position along the y-axis of the given actor.
