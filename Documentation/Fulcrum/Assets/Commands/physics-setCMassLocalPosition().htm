void FulcrumPhy::setCMassLocalPosition ( int id, float X, float Y, float Z )

Use this to set the centre of mass of your actor, lowering the centre of mass of an actor is a good way to stop it from toppling over. Its usually best to keep the centre of mass within the frame of the actor or as close to as possible, if its not you may get unpredictable results in certain situations.

Parameters:
int id - Object/Actor id.
float X - New centre of mass local position along the x-axis.
float Y - New centre of mass local position along the y-axis.
float Z - New centre of mass local position along the z-axis.

Returns:
